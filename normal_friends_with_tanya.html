Normal Friends with Tanya Goswami is an iconic (yet suspiciously underrated) friendship that has stood the test of time, snacks, and several Delhi heatwaves. This friendship is allegedly "normal" — although sources disagree on what exactly that means.[1]

History

The friendship began under mysterious but definitely very regular circumstances. Eyewitnesses claim it involved a cup of chai, a passive-aggressive meme, and a mutual love for dramatic exits.

Key Events

  • March 2023 – First brunch where nothing dramatic happened. (Allegedly.)
  • July 2024 – Tanya says "let’s do something normal" and books a trip to the hills with 14 strangers.
  • December 2024 – Both friends agree to “not talk for a bit” and accidentally set a world record for mutual ghosting.

Quotes

"I'm just saying, this is very normal behavior." – Tanya Goswami
"We are not weird, YOU are weird." – Anonymous friend, probably not Tanya

Legacy

The duo continues to redefine the meaning of 'normal friendship' by consistently doing things that are absolutely not normal. Cultural analysts remain confused.[2]
